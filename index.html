What if I lost my password?

Let's face it, everything needs a unique password these days. You probably have sticky notes all over your desk with them. Don't worry though, If you forget yours, just go to the Log In Page Select "Forgot Password" and we can send you a reset link.

‍

Where is my information stored?

Everybody wants your information these days. Not to worry. Your information will be safe and secure with us. We use Amazon Web Services (AWS) and Vercel to store all your information safely.

‍

What makes construction
professionals trust SB360?

Knowing that our software was designed by Builders for Builders is crucial because we genuinely understand your challenges. Unlike software engineers who may not have a deep understanding of our industry, our software was created by individuals who are passionate about construction.

Moreover, being endorsed as the Official construction software of The Building  Industry Association of Stark & East central Ohio further solidifies SM360's credibility and  reliability.

How do I cancel my subscription?

We hope you enjoy using Smart Builder 360. However, there may be instances where you need to cancel your subscription. Under the Settings tab, you'll find a red box labeled "Cancel Plan." Simply select it, and your subscription will be canceled. It's important to note the responsibility for canceling your plan lies solely with the user.
